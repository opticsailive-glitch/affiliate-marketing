Buying Guide

When shopping for a laptop under $500, consider these key factors:

  • Processor: Look for at least an AMD Ryzen 3 or Intel Core i3 for decent performance
  • RAM: 8GB is ideal for multitasking; 4GB is the minimum for basic use
  • Storage: SSD storage (even 128GB) is much faster than traditional hard drives
    • Display: Full HD (1920x1080) resolution provides a much better viewing experience than HD
  • Battery Life: Aim for at least 6-8 hours for portability
  • Operating System: Windows 11 Home is standard; consider Chromebooks if you primarily use web apps

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use a laptop under $500 for gaming?

Most laptops in this price range have integrated graphics that can handle very light gaming or older titles, but they're not suitable for modern AAA games. For gaming, you'd need to increase your budget significantly.

Are refurbished laptops a good option under $500?

Yes, refurbished laptops from reputable sellers can offer much better specifications for the same price. Look for warranties and return policies when buying refurbished.

How long will a budget laptop last?

With proper care, a budget laptop should last 3-5 years for everyday tasks. You may notice it slowing down as software becomes more demanding over time.
